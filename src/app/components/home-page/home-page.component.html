Our Story

Amanda and Christian's story begins with God preparing the way a week before they even knew each other’s name. God sent Christian to a coffee shop where a faithful soon to be friend, Jayci, invited him to a college church group called Hill City. Christian was excited at the opportunity but had little idea what God really had planned for him at this church group.

The following Wednesday, Amanda had been lead by God’s grace to the same church group for a few weeks and on this particular night, the happy couple was introduced by a mutual friend, Katie. The two began hanging out outside of church- but not without Christian catching feelings and Amanda friendzoning him immediately. It just so happened God was working a little slower in Amanda’s heart than He was in Christian's, until a few coffee dates and a high school church camping trip later, Amanda came around and they solidified their relationship with a fateful high five. Two years later, the couple is still sharing coffee and high fives and plan to do so for the rest of their lives.
